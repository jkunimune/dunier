tema dunier

dunier - tul da helpa kitaber, gemlider, e bashochaner bina veriki chansagi dunia da su zi chan abla bono rastu. le jinu falsozari sem e balibali param, e chutu yangi zemgraf, histori zamantable, e informi warke da mey desh e bash. no komputerprogram abla vica sukia e chanivia da holi proces da dunjang, a dunier abla handa fen da no mey insan abla o volu fata. le no holi janger, a le si samsifi ko hamar.

dunier kapa idey cha badi jener da fantazi zemgraf, ko le da azgar, a dunier may fokusa samsifia da veria. le min konativi ko ali jener, e le chana min lil detal, misalo loke da kuangia o politiki guanxe. a le si konsida multi detal, da nu ali jener e da abla vidu kandayo, misalo planeti kurvia, plati zemkaskia, e bashi mutia.

dunier su asle kode abil be vizi a github.com/jkunimune/dunier.

sabeteze

Dunier is made by Justin Kunimune, a Wikipedia editor and linguistics enthusiast. It is heavily inspired by Azgaar's Fantasy Map Generator. It makes use of Vladimir Agaforkin's Tinyqueue, Stefan Haack's Bootstrap Input Spinner, Axel Rauchmayer's Enumify, and James Hall's JSPDF, as well as Plotly and Bootstrap.
